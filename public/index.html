Novel Promotional Website

In 2014, I finished the first draft of my second novel, World Without Humanity. At the time, I was also getting aquainted with AngularJS, so I thought it would be a great learning experience to use Angular to create a promotional website for my novel.

The primary goal was to create interest in my writing, but I also wanted to provide real value to people in the process. To accomplish this, I focused the promotion on a blog that would explore the process of storytelling. The idea was to build an audience around the blog, which would eventually convert to book sales.

Ultimately, I didn't have time to actually run the blog. I did, however, finish building the website. I may use it in the future when I get around to editing my novel.
